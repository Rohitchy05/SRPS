The rules of Xtreme Rock Paper Scissors are very simple

You and the computer will begin with x number of every card

You will both choose to play a card and as usual rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock.

However, the loser will lose the card they played and the winner will gain that card. So, if you play rock and the computer plays paper then you lose a rock card and the computer gains a rock card.

In the event of a draw, both sides keep their cards and go again.

Both sides keep playing until one side runs out of cards.
